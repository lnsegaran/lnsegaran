Over the past couple of years I've been doing a lot with modular synthesisers. I decided to try my hand at designing and making my own Eurorack synthesiser.

I started with the idea that I would make a modualr drum machine and work progressively into bass and lead synths. The first few modules I made were all to do with clocks and gates, then I began to make some drum synth modules which would provide the individual drum sounds for the machine. I currently have a bass drum, two toms and two high hats.

The main drum modules are mostly analogue with a few digital components however most of the clock and gate signals are generated digitally using programmed arduinos.
